# Dialing in espresso: grind, dose, time

The whole game is one ratio and one clock: 18 g in, ~36 g out, in
25–30 seconds. Everything else adjusts toward that.

- **Shot runs fast + sour** → grind finer (extraction too low).
- **Shot drips + bitter** → grind coarser (extraction too high).
- Change **one variable per shot**, and purge a few grams after any
  grind change — the chamber still holds the old setting.

Current setting for the season's medium roast: 2.4 on the dial,
18.1 g dose, 27 s to 36 g. Beans a week off roast pull noticeably
faster than beans at three weeks; re-dial rather than chasing it with
dose.
