# Reading list — retrieval and ranking

Working queue, roughly ordered:

- Robertson & Zaragoza, *The Probabilistic Relevance Framework: BM25
  and Beyond* — the lexical baseline everything gets compared against.
- Cormack, Clarke & Buettcher, *Reciprocal Rank Fusion outperforms
  Condorcet and individual Rank Learning Methods* — three pages, one
  formula, still the fusion workhorse (see [[hybrid-retrieval]]).
- Muennighoff et al., *MTEB: Massive Text Embedding Benchmark* — how
  small embedding models earn their spot on modest hardware
  ([[embedded-vector-search]]).
- The SQLite documentation on FTS5 — external-content tables are the
  detail everyone skips and then rediscovers the hard way.
